Our Story

Founded by military veteran Matt Ramsey, MH Construction was born from the belief that construction should embody the same values learned in military service: precision, integrity, accountability, and unwavering commitment to excellence.

After serving our country, we brought military discipline and attention to detail to the construction industry. What started as a commitment to quality has evolved into a revolutionary approach that combines traditional craftsmanship with cutting-edge technology.

Today, MH Construction stands as the Pacific Northwest's most innovative construction company, setting new standards for transparency, client experience, and project execution. We're not just building structures – we're building the future of construction.
